The Michel Thomas Method
gemini-flash (gemini-2.5-flash)
TLDR

Adults can learn languages much faster than children due to their existing cognitive abilities and understanding of abstract concepts like grammar, which allows for rapid acquisition of conversational fluency.

Key points
  • The Michel Thomas method, developed by a Holocaust survivor and intelligence officer, claims to get adults to basic conversational fluency in a new language within a week.
  • Adults already possess a foundational understanding of labeling, concepts, and different types of abstraction, such as grammar, which can be directly explained and applied.
  • Unlike children, adults can grasp complex linguistic structures and rules, which significantly accelerates the learning process.
  • The common misconception that children learn languages faster stems from the fact that children are fully immersed and have no other responsibilities, not from superior learning ability.
Takeaway

Recognize that your adult cognitive abilities, including your understanding of abstract concepts and grammar, are powerful assets for rapid language acquisition. Don't fall for the myth that children are inherently better language learners; instead, leverage your existing knowledge to learn more efficiently.
