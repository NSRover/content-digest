For example, the channel frequently points to declining consumer spending at major retailers like Target and McDonald's, rising auto loan delinquencies, and plummeting new car sales among lower-income households as evidence of widespread consumer distress and income contraction.

The creator highlights issues like 'garbage lending,' inflated loan valuations, and increasing investor redemptions in private credit funds (e.g., BlackRock, Blue Owl, Morgan Stanley) as precursors to a credit crunch. The channel also frequently emphasizes the disconnect between stock market performance and the real economy, arguing that market highs often mask underlying weaknesses and are fueled by passive investing and debt-driven bubbles, such as the 'AI bubble.'
